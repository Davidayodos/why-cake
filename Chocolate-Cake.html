Chocolate Cake

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups (400 grams) granulated white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups (245 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup (75 grams) unsweetened cocoa powder (natural or Dutch-processed)
  • 1 cup (240 ml) warm water or coffee
  • Little sault
  • 6 ounces (180 grams) semi sweet or bittersweet chocolate

PREPARATION

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Lightly grease and flour your choice of pans: one 9" x 13" pan, two 9" round pans, or three 8" round pans. (To make a four-layer cake, use two 9" round pans; the layers will each be split in half.)
  2. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cocoa, and sugar into a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add the butter and mix at low speed for 1 minute.
  4. With the mixer running, add the oil and continue mixing until the mixture looks sandy, about 30 seconds more.
  5. In a large measuring cup, combine the vanilla with the milk and coffee (or water), and add all at once
  6. Bake for 34 minutes for a 9" x 13" pan, 28 to 30 minutes for 9" layers, and 24 to 26 minutes for 8" layers.
  7. Remove the cake from the oven and let cool completely in the pan(s) on a rack.
